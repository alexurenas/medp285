About Heatologist

I want to create an online space where I can provide three different sauces to people at different levels. Some will be for the hot-sauce-curious like me and others for people who are familiar but want to try something other than what’s shelved in a commercial supermarket. They might not be able to find these flavors in Buffalo Wild Wings but they will hopefully know which flavor profiles they are into which makes sauce selection easier.
